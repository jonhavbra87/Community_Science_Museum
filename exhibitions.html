explore together

Dinosaur Timeline

A captivating display showcasing the evolution of dinosaurs over millions of years, from their humble beginnings to their dominance and eventual extinction.

Dino Fossil Gallery

A curated collection of real dinosaur fossils and detailed replicas, providing insight into the diversity of these ancient creatures.

Dinosaur Anatomy

Interactive stations that allow visitors to explore the anatomy of different dinosaur species, from skeletal structure to unique adaptations.

Dino Dig Adventure

A hands-on experience where visitors can participate in a simulated archaeological dig, uncovering fossils and piecing together the history of dinosaurs.
