Exciting Lecture Series Alert!

Join Us for a Fascinating Exploration of Aeronautics and Beyond with Prof. Sheila Widnall from MIT!

Dates: 02. October, 03. October and 05. October 2023

Time: 10.00 to 14.00 - includes luchbreak

Get ready for an intellectual journey that transcends the skies! We are thrilled to announce a captivating lecture series by none other than Prof. Sheila Widnall, distinguished faculty member at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). Prepare to be enthralled as we delve into the evolution of aeronautics and the boundless horizons of 'space' exploration.

What to Expect

Lecture 1: "Pioneering the Skies: Aeronautics Through Time" Discover the remarkable journey of aeronautics development, from the earliest flight attempts to the cutting-edge technology of today. Prof. Widnall will take you through pivotal moments, innovations, and the individuals who shaped the course of flight.

Lecture 2: "Breaking Gravity's Chains: Into the World of Aerospace Engineering" Explore the captivating realm of aerospace engineering with insights into designing aircraft and spacecraft. Prof. Widnall will unravel the engineering marvels that enable us to conquer gravity and reach for the stars.

Lecture 3: "Frontiers of Exploration: A Glimpse into the Future of 'Space'" Embark on an extraordinary journey into the future of space exploration. Discover how emerging technologies, collaborations, and visionary concepts are shaping the possibilities of human space travel and beyond

About Prof. Sheila Widnall: Prof. Widnall's profound expertise in aeronautics, coupled with her extensive research and contributions, have earned her a prominent place in the aerospace field. Her lectures promise to deliver a blend of historical insights, cutting-edge knowledge, and inspiring visions.
